Overview OF ISTE-KKWIEER

It’s a national, professional, non-profit society which was registered under the Indian Societies Registration Act. Established with the aim of fostering excellence in engineering and technology education, it stands as the sole national organization dedicated to this cause. Today, the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) boasts a robust membership, comprising over 5.5 lakh student members, 1 lakh teacher members, and two thousand four hundred institutional members, including our esteemed institute.

The overarching goals of ISTE encompass a range of objectives aimed at enhancing technical education across the nation. These objectives include formulating the goals and responsibilities of technical education, ensuring effective linkage between industries, technical institutions, and society, and recognizing and rewarding excellence in the field. To this end, ISTE plays a pivotal role in awarding honorary fellowships, as well as prestigious awards and prizes, furthering the objectives of technical education and promoting innovation and expertise in the field.
